      `bash ${CLAUDE_PLUGIN_ROOT}/scripts/gh-search.sh "<query>"`

    Collect 5 JSON arrays.

    ## Step 4: Dedup + Rank (per D-10)
    Apply the "Dedup & Ranking" rule from query-patterns.md. Compute rank-sum.

    ## Step 5: Verify (per D-11, T1-04, T1-05)
    For each of the 5 ranked candidates, run:
      `bash ${CLAUDE_PLUGIN_ROOT}/scripts/verify-repo.sh "<owner/repo>"`

    per Pitfall 8). Drop any candidate with non-zero exit (404). Collect verified

    Collect axis_scores per candidate. DERIVE candidate_verdict mechanically

      `bash ${CLAUDE_PLUGIN_ROOT}/scripts/staleness.sh "<verified-json>"`
